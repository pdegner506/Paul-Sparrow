Audio-Visual Productions & Multi-Image Experiences

The phrase "Multi-Media" has come to mean different things to different people. As a photographer I use still photography exclusively and incorporate dynamic musical soundtracks along with image movements and animated sequences to create a cinematic journey that takes the imagery beyond just the individual photographs.

The goal of these audio-visual experiences is to arouse in the audience an emotional response to the images beyond just what is seen and make the audience really "experience" them in a way not present in the images themselves.

Each show is formatted in the wide screen format and uses state of the art high definition digital projection to maintain the utmost image quality. Audio is supplied by a 5 speaker surround system to create an audio experience that equals the dynamic images projected. The extremely detailed and rich imagery coupled with the large sound creates for the viewer an extraordinary sensory experience.

I've had the opportunity to present my shows at many major photography conferences and conventions including; the Latow International Audio-Visual Festival, the GTCCC "Capturing Light & Shadows" conference, Canada Camera Conference, New England Camera Club Council Convention, Niagara Regional Camera Clubs Convention, Professional Photographers of Ontario, and many other photography organizations across Canada.
