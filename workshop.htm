Photography Workshops

Are you just starting out with your first camera... getting back into taking pictures... or an experienced photographer looking for new challenges?

Join me for one of my classes and let me guide you through the world of photography to help you "make" better pictures.

With over 35 years of professional experience these in depth workshops will take you through the basics and then let you further explore your photography.

Digital Photography 101

Learn the basics of how to control your camera and go beyond "point & shoot" to make your pictures more than just a snap shot.

Digital Photography 102

Each week explore the world of photography and learn the best ways to shoot the most common styles of photography.

Photoshop Elements

Learn the techniques used in the manipulation and enhancement of images in the digital medium using Photoshop Elements where the image is just the starting point.

Weekly handout reference material is included with each course.
