Welcome...

As a photographer I try to capture both the natural and man made world and my intention is to use the medium in unique and imaginative ways that have a strong visual impact. Through the use of the audio-visual medium I am also exploring ideas that tell a story or reveal a personal idea or concept that I've captured with my photography. My goal is to arouse in the viewer an emotional response to the images beyond just what is seen, to make the audience really "experience" them, and go beneath their surface.
